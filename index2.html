Cognitive Continuity

“Don’t Drop the Patient”

Healthcare is increasingly fragmented as patients shuttle between clinics, hospitals, and their own homes to receive the most appropriate and cost-effective care. As patients move between contexts, gaps in care, errors, and miscommunication often occur. We are studying how to reduce these adverse events by building tools that help patients and providers maintain *cognitive continuity*.
